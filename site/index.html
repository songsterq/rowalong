What it does

A coach in the corner, not another screen to mind.

01

Floats over anything

Rides above browser video and native macOS fullscreen apps, the same window staying put whether you're in a streaming tab or a full-screen player.

02

Workouts, generated

Warm-up, work pushes, and cool-down built for you in 10, 20, or 30 minutes. Pick a push strategy, tweak any block, and save your own as a template.

03

Stroke-rate coaching

Each interval shows a recommended stroke rate with a live drive / recovery pace bar to row against, plus tone cues on every transition so you can keep your eyes on the show.

04

Stays out of the way

Shrink it to a minimal pill of just the numbers, or open coach mode for the full readout. Click to pause, drag it to any corner.

05

Local and private

Runs entirely on your Mac. No account, no tracking, nothing leaves the machine. Templates and preferences are stored locally.

How it works

Three steps, then row.

Step 01

Build a session

Choose a length and strategy, or load a saved template. Fine-tune the blocks if you like.

Step 02

Start your show

Press start. The overlay lifts out into its own always-on-top window over whatever's playing.

Step 03

Follow the pace

Match the stroke bar, watch the countdown, listen for the cues. Click to pause any time.
